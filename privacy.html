KhetMap Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Information We Collect

  • Satellite imagery data for your agricultural fields (loaded from public satellite APIs)
  • Field boundaries and crop data you create (stored locally in your browser)

How We Use Your Data

We use your data to provide satellite-based crop health monitoring, water analysis, and field management for your agricultural lands. All data is processed locally in your browser.

Data Storage

Your field data is stored locally in your browser using localStorage. No data is sent to any external server. Satellite imagery is loaded from public APIs (NASA GIBS, ESRI, OpenStreetMap) and is subject to their respective terms of service.

Third-Party Services

KhetMap loads map tiles and satellite imagery from:

  • ESRI — Satellite basemap tiles
  • OpenStreetMap — Street map tiles & Nominatim search
  • NASA GIBS — NDVI, NDWI, and SAVI imagery

These services may collect usage data according to their own privacy policies.
